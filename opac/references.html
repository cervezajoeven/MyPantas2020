Tips on how to search
  1. Academic researches or Coursework - theses, dissertations, case studies, special problems...
  2. Articles - Index of periodical articles from local and foreign journals.
  3. Book literature or Analytic - index of selected articles, essays, short stories, poetry and papers coming from books and anthologies.
  4. Books - electronic catalogs of book collection.
  5. Cases and jurisprudence - index of selected cases and jurisprudence coming from Supreme Court Rule Annotated.
  6. Electronic resources - catalog of electronic materials and digital materials (e-books, e-articles, mp3, mp4, ppt...)
  7. Special materials and Audio-Visual resources - catalog of non-print materials like DVD, VHS, CD-ROM, and other special materials.
  8. Vertical or Information files - index of selected clippings and other loose leaf materials.
  9. Serials or Bound periodials - catalog information of bound periodicals and its holdings.
  1. Select a type of material from radio button list.
  2. Type your topic, subject, or keywords inside the input box.
  3. Then, click submit button to submit your queries.
